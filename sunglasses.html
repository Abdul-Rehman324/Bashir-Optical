Why Quality Sunglasses Matter

Wearing quality sunglasses isn't just about styleâ€”it's about protecting your eyes from harmful UV radiation that can contribute to various eye problems, including:

  • Cataracts: Long-term UV exposure can increase the risk of cataracts
  • Macular Degeneration: UV light may contribute to this serious eye condition
  • Photokeratitis: Essentially a sunburn of the eye, causing temporary pain and vision problems
  • Pterygium: A growth on the eye's surface that can cause irritation and affect vision
  • Skin Cancer: The delicate skin around your eyes is vulnerable to sun damage
