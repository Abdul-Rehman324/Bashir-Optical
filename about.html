About Us

Our journey of excellence since 1980

Our Story

Founded in 1980, Bashir Optical has been a trusted name in eye care for over four decades. What started as a small family business has grown into a premium optical center known for its exceptional service and quality products.

Our founder, Mr. Nasim Sajid, had a vision to provide affordable yet high-quality eye care solutions to the community. His dedication to customer satisfaction and professional ethics formed the foundation of our business values that we continue to uphold today.

Over the years, we have continuously evolved, incorporating the latest technology and techniques in optometry while maintaining our commitment to personalized care.

1980

The Beginning

Bashir Optical was established as a small shop with a commitment to providing quality eyewear.

2000

Expansion

We expanded our services to include comprehensive eye examinations and a wider range of premium frames.

2010

Technological Advancement

Introduced state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and digital lens technology.

2020

International Brands

Partnered with leading international eyewear brands to offer designer collections.

2025

Modern Era

Embraced digital platforms and introduced online services while maintaining our tradition of excellence in personal care.
