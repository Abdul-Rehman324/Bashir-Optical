Frame Repairs & Adjustments

Quick and professional frame repair and adjustment services by skilled technicians

Professional Repair Services

At Your Optical Center, we understand that your eyeglasses are an important part of your daily life. Our team of skilled technicians provides professional frame repair and adjustment services to ensure your eyewear fits comfortably and functions properly. Whether you need a simple adjustment or a complete frame repair, we have the expertise and tools to help.

Our Repair Services

Frame Adjustments
  • Temple Adjustments: Correct the fit behind your ears
  • Screw Replacement: Fix loose or missing screws
  • Nose Pad Adjustments: Ensure comfort and proper positioning
  • Nose Pad Replacement: Replace worn or missing nose pads
  • Frame Alignment: Make sure your glasses sit properly on your face

Benefits of Professional Frame Repairs

  • Save Money: Repairing your existing frames can be more economical than purchasing new ones
  • Extend Lifespan: Proper repairs can significantly extend the life of your eyewear
  • Improve Comfort: Professional adjustments ensure your glasses fit properly and comfortably
  • Maintain Vision Quality: Properly aligned frames ensure optimal vision correction
  • Preserve Favorite Frames: Keep wearing the frames you love even after damage

Frame Care Tips

  • Always use both hands when removing your glasses
  • Store your glasses in a protective case when not wearing them
  • Clean your frames regularly with lens cleaner and a microfiber cloth
  • Avoid leaving your glasses in hot environments (like a car dashboard)
  • Remove your glasses before playing sports or engaging in physical activities
  • Visit us for regular adjustments and maintenance
