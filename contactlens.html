Our Contact Lens Fitting Process

  1. Comprehensive Eye Exam: Before fitting contact lenses, we conduct a thorough eye examination to check your vision and eye health
  2. Contact Lens Consultation: We discuss your lifestyle, preferences, and eye health to determine the best type of contact lenses for you
  3. Precise Measurements: We measure your cornea's curvature, pupil size, and tear film quality to ensure proper fit
  4. Trial Lenses: We provide trial lenses to test comfort and vision quality before finalizing your prescription
  5. Insertion and Removal Training: For new wearers, we provide comprehensive training on how to properly insert, remove, and care for your lenses
  6. Follow-up Care: We schedule a follow-up appointment to evaluate how your eyes are adapting to the contact lenses

Contact Lens Care Tips

  • Always wash hands thoroughly before handling contact lenses
  • Clean and store reusable lenses in fresh solution every night
  • Never sleep in your contact lenses unless specifically designed for extended wear
  • Replace your contact lens case every three months
  • Never use tap water or saliva on your lenses
  • Follow the recommended replacement schedule for your specific lenses
  • Remove lenses immediately if you experience redness, pain, or blurred vision
  • Attend regular check-ups to monitor your eye health
