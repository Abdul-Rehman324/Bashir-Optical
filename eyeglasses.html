Our Collection

At Your Optical Center, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse range of high-quality eyeglasses to suit every style, need, and budget. Our collection features frames from leading international designers and brands, ensuring you'll find the perfect pair to complement your look and lifestyle.

Designer Frames

We carry an extensive selection of designer frames from top brands including:

  • Ray-Ban: Classic styles with superior quality
  • Gucci: Luxury fashion frames with distinctive details
  • Prada: Sophisticated designs with modern flair
  • Versace: Bold, statement-making frames
  • Oakley: Performance-oriented sporty frames
  • Tom Ford: Elegant, refined designs
  • Emporio Armani: Contemporary, stylish frames
  • Burberry: Classic British designs with modern elements

Frame Materials

Our frames are crafted from a variety of high-quality materials to ensure durability, comfort, and style:

  • Titanium: Lightweight, durable, and hypoallergenic
  • Acetate: Versatile, colorful, and comfortable
  • Stainless Steel: Strong, corrosion-resistant, and sleek
  • Carbon Fiber: Ultra-lightweight and extremely durable
  • Eco-friendly Materials: Sustainable options made from recycled or bio-based materials

Premium Lenses

We offer a wide range of premium lens options to meet your specific vision needs:

Lens Materials
  • CR-39 Plastic: Standard lightweight plastic lenses
  • Polycarbonate: Impact-resistant lenses ideal for children and active adults
  • High-Index: Thinner, lighter lenses for stronger prescriptions
  • Trivex: Lightweight, impact-resistant lenses with excellent optical clarity
Lens Coatings & Treatments
  • Anti-Reflective: Reduces glare and eye strain
  • Blue Light Filter: Protects eyes from digital screen exposure
  • Scratch-Resistant: Improves durability and longevity
  • UV Protection: Blocks harmful ultraviolet rays
  • Photochromic: Transitions from clear to dark when exposed to sunlight
  • Polarized: Reduces glare from reflective surfaces
