Eye Checkup Services

Comprehensive eye examination by certified professionals using advanced technology

Dr. Zubair Hamid

Senior Optometrist & Consultant

Dr. Zubair Hamid specializes in comprehensive eye care at our optical center. With over 15 years of experience, he provides expert diagnosis and treatment for various eye conditions to patients of all ages.

Eye Conditions Treated

Dr. Zubair Hamid provides diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of eye conditions including:

Refractive Errors
  • Myopia (Nearsightedness): Difficulty seeing distant objects clearly
  • Hyperopia (Farsightedness): Difficulty seeing close objects clearly
  • Astigmatism: Blurred or distorted vision at all distances
  • Presbyopia: Age-related difficulty focusing on close objects
Common Eye Diseases
  • Glaucoma: Elevated pressure in the eye that can damage the optic nerve
  • Cataracts: Clouding of the eye's lens causing blurry vision
  • Macular Degeneration: Deterioration of the central portion of the retina
  • Diabetic Retinopathy: Damage to blood vessels in the retina due to diabetes
Eye Infections & Conditions
  • Conjunctivitis (Pink Eye): Inflammation of the conjunctiva
  • Dry Eyes: Insufficient tear production or poor tear quality
  • Computer Vision Syndrome: Eye strain and discomfort from digital screen use
  • Eye Allergies: Inflammation of the eyes due to allergens

Our Eye Examination Process

Our comprehensive eye examinations include:

  • Visual Acuity Testing: Measures how well you can see at various distances
  • Refraction Assessment: Determines your exact prescription needs
  • Eye Pressure Test: Screens for glaucoma
  • Visual Field Test: Checks for blind spots in your vision
  • Retinal Examination: Evaluates the health of the back of your eye
  • Slit Lamp Examination: Detailed view of the eye's structures
